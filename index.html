ABOUT

UNSPKBLE is a rock band from southern France who delivers radical and melodic post-punk tunes, drifting towards New wave, Cold wave, and sometimes even Noise rock. According to reviews of their debut EP "FRICTION" (June 2020, Rejuvenation Records/Kerviniou Recordz), UNSPKBLE's music evokes the soundscapes of KILLING JOKE, THE SOUND, CHRISTIAN DEATH, PLAY DEAD, SIOUXSIE & THE BANSHEES, BAUHAUS, but also more recent formations like SHAME, SOFT KILL or WHISPERING SONS.
